Color Scheme

This section describes which colors will apply to elements and content

Body: The Body will have a descendant color of the Antique White color that is rgb(252, 246, 239)to combine in the background with what the logo shows and give it seriousness and elegance at the same time.

H1: This type of headings will have the color rgb (56, 51, 45), in affinity with the main colors of the logo and the characteristics of the company.

The H2 to H6 will have the color #304352 which is a descendant of the main blue that concatenates with the main theme and the logo.

The other elements such as images, shapes and utilities combine these colors and derived from them along with the gold that also comes from the proposed theme as well as in the logo.
