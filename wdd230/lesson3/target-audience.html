Target Audience

The target audience of this chamber of commerce is small, medium and large entrepreneurs throughout the country who are looking for the best growth opportunities, mainly, it is also aimed at business students and those who are passionate about finance who want to grow in an environment full of successful people and with a wonderful vision.

Personas

Persona 1: A young male IT specialist , about 23 years old. Not married. He was educated in Moscow in the field of IT, then worked for two years in London at Google. Then he moved to Tbilisi to start his own startup with friends. He uses a macbook, he constantly uses the Internet, as it is related to his work, about 4-8 hours a day.

Persona 2: The man, about 51 years old, Georgian, has lived in Tbilisi all his life. He inherited vineyards from his father and made a medium-sized business on this. Produces wine for the domestic market. Higher education in agro-industrial sphere. Married. Two children. Uses his laptop to surf the Internet for about 2-3 hours a day.:
