Purpose

The Path of the Stars Chamber of Commerce represents the interests of a broad spectrum of organizations from the private, nonprofit, academic, and public sectors, including the business community, job creators, and innovators across the country. Our mission is to design and promote opportunities and solutions for a prosperous general economy that is inclusive and globally competitive. With us, companies are more likely to take their business to the highest economic levels since we have strategies that adapt to all types of business without any limits. In us you could find a group that always looks to the future and achieves its goals with commitment.
