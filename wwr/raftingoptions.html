Main Fork of the Salmon River, Idaho

Nestled deep in the Frank Church Wilderness of No Return winds the Salmon river. The Salmon was given the nickname long ago as the River of No Return by the people living near it because of the number of folks that came to visit and fell so in love with the rugged beauty of the river that they never left. Herds of elk and deer graze on the hillsides around the river and bear and bighorn sheep are also commonly sighted. The river corridor is also lined with awe inspiring ponderosa pine which tower over the sandy beaches in the campsites and provide shelter from sun or rain when needed. Let's not forget the rapids! They range from class I-IV. The Salmon is one of the longest undammed rivers in the United States. As a free flowing river the water is higher, faster, and colder in the early summer and lower, slower, and warmer in the late summer. There are good rapids all summer...but families with children who like to swim tend to appreciate the late summer's slower, warmer water more. And like this there are many other wonders that will be within your reach just by contacting us.

Camping

Near the river you choose you will always have the opportunity to find a nearby camp where you can spend the night before continuing with the adventure.

What can you find in our camps? We will help you with your tent, you will have camping food and you can also make your own inn with completely natural materials that we will also provide so that the experience continues to be as close to nature as possible. The funniest thing in our camps is that you will have time to share experiences with the group because we will put at your disposal a night campfire in which we will share and also show our experiences with those who are in the adventure so that we can have new ideas for the next day. and so add those new skills learned together. We want you to stay so we will put at your disposal the best of an overnight camp to turn your experience into a family day that we are sure you will want to experience more than once.

Stanley has lodging that will meet and exceed your expectations and fit your needs.
